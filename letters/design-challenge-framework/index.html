Design challenge framework

Let’s approach this in a structured way. Time for a framework.
How may people ____ (activity) in ____ (system) using ____ (tech)?
Activity. System. Tech. AST.
With these three elements, we can generate plenty of combinations of future design challenges.

Activity

As long as we’re alive, humans will still be doing things, the mundane everyday things that comprise how we interact with each other and the world around us. Communicate. Collaborate. Work. Shop. Learn. Trust. Love. Analyze. Eat. Commute. Rest. Explore. Escape.

System

The various structures, arenas, or industries that we have built up around us or contexts that we find ourselves in. The big things. Health. Water. Education. Government. Insurance. Wealth. Communication. Fashion. Factionalism. Food. Sports. War.

Tech

The various technology innovations that are projected to arrive in the coming decades. Augmented reality. Artificial intelligence. Quantum computing. On-demand printing. Kinetic surfaces. Space travel. Nanotechnology.
