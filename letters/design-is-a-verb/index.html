Design is a verb

Ok. I can’t avoid writing a book about the future of design without attempting to define the fuzzy word of design. Hundreds or thousands of people have attempted this. Here’s my take.

The problem is, “design” is a difficult word.

Design, the noun.

Design, the verb.

Design, the industry.

Design, the role.

“Design” is a fuzzy word.

Defining the damn thing

Bucky Fuller: comprehensive anticipatory designer

Design is one of the few words that means the activity and the outcome and the organization that delivers it.

Design is about the before and after. Having a common understanding of the before. Having an intentional target for the after. And where we are, right Now,

This book will intentionally avoid addressing the dreaded question of “what is design?” Too many pixels have been spilled over that and I personally don’t find it productive. However, what I do find more interesting than “design” the noun is “design” the verb.

Designing is bringing order to chaos.
