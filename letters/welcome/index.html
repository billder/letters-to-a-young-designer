Letters to a young designer

What may the future look like for today’s young designers, in the 2050s, a world difficult for us to imagine today?

This project centers on that one single question, naturally branching to other big questions,

Along the way we’ll look at

  • the history of design roles
  • how changing technology affected them
  • projected future technologies
  • increasing societal challenges
  • what is “design”
  • and what can you do about it

If I leave you with just three things, it’s this:

  • Your career, peaking in the 2050s, will have opportunities difficult for us to imagine today.
  • The world of design is much bigger than pixels that fit in rectangles.
  • Design can make people’s lives better. It can also make them much worse. Considering the state of the world, let’s focus on better, or at least not worse.

Let’s get started.
