Letters to a young designer

This project is a love letter to young designers. It anchors on one “simple” question…

What challenges may today’s young designers face in 20 years when they are tomorrow’s design leaders?

If this means you, my goal is to provide you with some context on the career of a designer and speculate what new opportunities you may have. Your generation will have an immense burden and incredible opportunity to change the world. Design can make people’s lives better. It can also make them much worse. Considering the state of the world, let’s make it better.

(And of course, if you’re not a young designer, you’re welcome as well.)

The main idea:

  • Today’s young designers will be at the peak of their careers in the 2040s and 2050s. That may sound like science fiction, but it will be their reality.
  • Until then, the world is likely to face both global problems and technological upheaval. This also means that design opportunities will be radically different from today, just as they have been in every recent decade.
  • To understand this, we’ll look at the history of design, understand how we arrived at today, and then speculate on what may happen.

Let’s get started.
