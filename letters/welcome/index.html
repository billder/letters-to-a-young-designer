Letters to a young designer

This project is a love letter to young designers. In here I aim to give young designers a past and potential future context of this profession. The next generation will have an immense burden and incredible opportunity to affect the world. Design can make people’s lives better. It can also make them much worse. Considering the state of the world, let’s focus on better, or at least not worse.

What opportunities may exist in the coming decades for today’s young designers?

This project centers on that one single question, naturally branching to other big questions,

The core points:

  • Today’s young designers will be at the peak of their careers in the 2040s and 2050s. That may sound like science fiction, but it will be their reality.
  • Their career opportunities will be great, based on increasing combinations of technology evolution and societal challenges.
  • We can project how future technologies may create design challenges by looking to the past and the history of design roles.
  • “Design” is hard word to pin down.
  • The world of design is much bigger than pixels that fit in rectangles.

Let’s get started.
