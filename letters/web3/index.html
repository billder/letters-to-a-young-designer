web123 is a progression of share, scale, trust

“This was a great read Chris, thanks. It’s still not clear to me what real problem web3 is trying to solve. It’s an engineering-centric approach to… what problem? Even talking about web123 as pendulum swings between de/centralized or client/server is an engineering-oriented frame. The progression of web123 to me is share, scale, trust. I get that web3 is going after trust, but I’d rather see it focus on trusting the content rather than validating a transaction. My thoughts are clearly a work in progress and may be for a while.”
