Technology generations

We can group generations by the technology that was “normal” in their baseline reality. Let’s say that a technology is baseline if it is widely available when a kid is five years old, as they are becoming aware of the world.

The web exploded in 1995 and was prevalent by 2000. So kids born in 1995 and on are part of the web generation. Mobile phones exploded in 2007 with the introduction of the iPhone but were also around a bit earlier. Let’s mark 2002 as the beginning of the mobile generation. And so on.
